About Me

I'm a Front-End Developer based in Odense, Denmark. I'm currently working at FamilyTricks - an app that helps families organize daily tasks while motivating kids and tweens to participate.

I am Co-Founder of Ruth Calendar, an accessible digital planner designed in collaboration with people with low vision. Ruth Calendar is dedicated my grandmother Ruth, who lost most of her eyesight due to an eye disease two years ago.

Beyond my work as a coder, I love spending time with family and friends, reading books and going on long 'coffee' walks.
