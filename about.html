About Me

I'm a Product Designer (UX/UI) based in Odense, Denmark. I am passionate about crafting impactful and accessible digital experiences. Besides working in design I have experience from several projects implementing designs in HTML & CSS.

I am Co-Founder and Lead Designer at Ruth Calendar, an accessible digital planner designed in collaboration with people with low vision. Ruth Calendar is dedicated my grandmother Ruth, who lost most of her eyesight due to an eye disease two years ago.

Beyond my work as a designer, I love spending time with family and friends, reading books, going on long 'coffee' walks, and sketching in my notebooks.
