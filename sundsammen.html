SUND SAMMEN

As a part of a real-life project during the bachelor 'Digital Concept Development' we collborated with UCL and the Steno Diabetes Center in Denmark to create a concept with the goal of helping and motivating busy parents with small children to achieve a healthier everyday life. The concept was built around a health and wellness app with available mini-courses that the user could pick and choose from according to their schedule and motivation level.

Derudover tilbydes brugeren adgang til en række podcasts, opskrifter o.lign. som alt sammen skal hjælpe dem i en sundere retning. Pga. efterspørgsel fra brugeren om podcasts om emner der passer til deres livssituation. Derudover er der hjælp til parforholdet. THE PROBLEM

Parents of young children often have a very busy everyday life and therefore lack the resources and mental energy to prioritize their their own health. As a result they have a tendency to end up with a liftestyle based on what is easiest and least time-consuming.

Most health and wellness apps on the market today are targeted a group who have a more flexible everyday life and who have more time to carry out fixed training programmes, than parents of small children do.
