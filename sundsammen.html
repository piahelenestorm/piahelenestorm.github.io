SUND SAMMEN

As a part of a real-life project during the bachelor 'Digital Concept Development' we collborated with UCL and the Steno Diabetes Center in Denmark to create a concept with the goal of helping and motivating busy parents with small children to achieve a healthier everyday life. The concept was built around a health and wellness app with available mini-courses that the user could pick and choose from according to their schedule and motivation level.

Derudover tilbydes brugeren adgang til en række podcasts, opskrifter o.lign. som alt sammen skal hjælpe dem i en sundere retning. Pga. efterspørgsel fra brugeren om podcasts om emner der passer til deres livssituation. Derudover er der hjælp til parforholdet.

Project duration

April 2023 - June 2023

Client

UCL + the Steno Diabetes Center

My role

As a part of a team of 4 user experience researchers and designers, my role included:
User Experience • Visual Design • Design System • Prototyping • Usability Testing
